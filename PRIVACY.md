# SmartSummary Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 31, 2026**

## What data we collect

SmartSummary reads the text content of web pages **only when you explicitly click the summarize button or press Alt+S**. This text is sent to our server to generate a summary using AI.

## How we use your data

- Page text is sent to our server solely for the purpose of generating a summary
- Page text is **not stored** on our servers after the summary is generated
- We do not track your browsing history
- We do not collect personal information unless you create a Pro account (email for subscription management only)

## Data storage

- Daily usage count is stored locally in your browser (chrome.storage)
- Subscription status is stored locally for performance
- No page content is permanently stored on our servers

## Third-party services

- **Google Gemini API**: Used to generate summaries. Page text is sent to Google's API. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Stripe**: Used for payment processing if you upgrade to Pro. See [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy)
